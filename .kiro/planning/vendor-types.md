## üìÖ Daily Menu System

### How It Works
1. **Morning Update** (by 9 AM)
   - Vendors update today's menu
   - Set available quantities
   - Mark unavailable items

2. **Customer Ordering** (9 AM - 11 AM)
   - Customers browse fresh menus
   - Place orders for lunch
   - Cutoff time: 11 AM

3. **Preparation** (11 AM - 12 PM)
   - Vendors prepare confirmed orders
   - Pack meals
   - Ready for pickup

4. **Delivery** (12 PM - 2 PM)
   - Dabbawalas collect orders
   - Deliver to customers
   - Lunch time delivery

### Menu Flexibility
- Change menu daily based on ingredients
- Seasonal variations
- Festival specials
- Customer preferences
- Leftover management
